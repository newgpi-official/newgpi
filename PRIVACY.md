# NewGPI Privacy Notice for Public Developer Materials

## Scope

This notice applies to the public documentation and example materials in this repository. Product-specific privacy terms must be published on the official website before a public service, API, SDK or demo collects personal data.

## Repository interactions

GitHub may process information associated with repository visits, stars, forks, issues, discussions and contributions under GitHub's own terms and privacy statement.

Do not submit personal data, account credentials, private identifiers, financial information or confidential business information through public issues or discussions.

## Examples and demos

Official examples should use synthetic or test data. Public demos should explain what data is collected, why it is needed, how long it is retained and how users can exercise applicable privacy rights.

## Product privacy policy

This repository document is not a substitute for the product privacy policy. The official policy URL and privacy contact will be added after they are published and approved on https://newgpi.vip.

## Updates

Material changes to this document will be visible in the repository commit history.
